What are we?

Project Invernia is a long-standing initiative to provide the MORDHAU Roleplaying experience that many can be involved in, and many more can enjoy. If you exist to fight your enemies or to involve yourself in story-rich quests and world-building plots where the intrigue is present - this place is for you.


What will you find?

  1. Aeternis Armoury and Customizations.
  2. Civilian and Military RP are encouraged.
  3. Faction and Individual playstyles co-exist.
  4. Quests, Storylines and Adventures for your enjoyment.
  5. Economy and War Systems made possible with the use of a Discord Bot.
  6. Hand-crafted, interactive lore designed by veterans of thousands of hours in DnD.
  7. Masterfully-crafted and huge in size map, players have a degree of input on how it is shaped through the Economy system.
  8. Gamemasters work in tandem with the players to carefully tailor events that suit the individual needs and settings for the stories.
